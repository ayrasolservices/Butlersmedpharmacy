Traditional Remedies & Their Uses

Digestive & Stomach Remedies

  • Manzanilla (Chamomile): For upset stomach, indigestion, and colic
  • Boldo Leaf: Liver support and digestive aid
  • Hierbabuena (Mint): Relieves gas and nausea
  • Papaya Leaf: For parasites and digestion
  • Aloe Vera (Sábila): Soothes stomach ulcers and inflammation

Respiratory & Cold Care

  • Eucalyptus: For coughs and congestion
  • Gordolobo: Expectorant for chest congestion
  • Oregano Oil: Natural antibiotic for infections
  • Canela (Cinnamon): Warm tea for colds and flu
  • Jengibre (Ginger): For sore throat and inflammation

Pain & Inflammation Relief

  • Árnica: Creams and gels for bruises and muscle pain
  • Romero (Rosemary): Oil for joint and muscle pain
  • Albahaca (Basil): Headache and tension relief
  • Caléndula: Skin inflammation and wound healing
  • Menta (Peppermint): Topical pain relief

Cultural Health Traditions

  • Susto (Fright) Remedies: Herbal blends for shock or trauma
  • Empacho Treatments: For digestive blockages in children
  • Mal de Ojo (Evil Eye): Protective rituals and remedies
  • Bilis (Bile) Treatments: For anger-related ailments
  • Nervios (Nerves): Calming herbs for anxiety
